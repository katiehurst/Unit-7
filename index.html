SCRAP PHX

What is SCRAP PHX?

SCRAP PHX is a creative reuse store and donation center. Along with the ever-changing and diverse store items, SCRAP also provides a range of educational opportunities within the community and hosts local reuse-based artists in our Gallery.

Interested in Getting Involved?

SCRAP PHX offers “Drop-In” hours every Thursday from 11am-4pm for those who would like to give volunteering with us a try before going through our application and interview process. Drop in for as little or as long as you would like within these hours and you will be provided with a project and instructions on how to complete it. Be sure to wear closed-toed shoes and clothes you don’t mind getting dusty!

You can also email katie@katiehurst.com to get connected to specific volunteering opportunities!
